Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

Welcome to GoodCalc. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how GoodCalc handles your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

GoodCalc is designed as a local-first productivity tool. We focus on providing a powerful calculation experience without compromising your personal data.

  • User-Defined Categories and Buttons: All calculation structures, categories, and custom buttons you create are stored locally on your device.
  • Importing Data (Excel/Files): When you import data via Excel files, the app processes this data locally to generate your calculation interface. We do not upload your spreadsheets to any external servers.
  • QR Code Integration: The app may use your device’s camera to scan QR Codes for importing configurations. Camera access is used solely for scanning and is not used to record or transmit photos or videos.

2. Data Storage

We do not operate a centralized database for user content. Your data stays where it belongs—on your device. If you delete the app, your locally stored configurations and data will be removed unless you have performed a manual backup.

3. Permissions

To provide full functionality, GoodCalc may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: Used exclusively to scan QR codes for data and configuration import.
  • Storage/Files: Used to read Excel files or document files for importing custom calculation categories.

4. Third-Party Services

GoodCalc does not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not use third-party tracking or analytics that identify you personally.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us access to your device’s local files and camera. We strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it, but remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
